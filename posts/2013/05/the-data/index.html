The data

serycjon

2013-05-05 17:29

Source

Hi there :)
Last time I wrote mainly about the recycle bins database. Unfortunatelly Prazske Sluzby a.s. refused to give me access to it :(
My next step was to try and obtain the data from their interactive online map, but I was not succesfull as their system is quite leak-proof. The map is sent in form of png images, so you can't get bins coordinates. When you click on the map, there is a request sent to their server. If you clicked on recycle bin, you get response with more information about it... I see no easy way to get in... :( Searching by address is not any more helpfull neither.

For now, I continue developing my app with random data, but I will probably have to copy them manually :-/

That's for the sad news. And now for the good ones. I managed to make filter dialog allowing user to choose types of waste (paper, glass, plastic and Tetra Pak's) to be shown. It isn't perfect from the graphical point of view, but one of the key features is finally working :)

Next working feature is limiting of camera view. Markers are shown only if zoom is greater than 13 and if user zooms out too much or if he pan Prague out of the view he is smoothly returned back and informed by "Toast"
