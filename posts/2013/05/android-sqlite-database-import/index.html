serycjon

It is pretty hard to get sqlite database created on PC to work in your Android app, but if you keep googling and googling, you will eventually find this very nice blogpost:

http://www.reigndesign.com/blog/using-your-own-sqlite-database-in-android-applications/
It helped me really much! I had to correct some bugs in it, but it is almost perfect. Highly recommended for anyone trying to get his DB of recycle bins into his Android app :D
